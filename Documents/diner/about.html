About Berwick Manor

Berwick Manor is more than just a restaurant and shisha lounge; it's a destination where culinary excellence meets a vibrant social atmosphere. Our story began with a vision to create a space where guests could savor exquisite dishes, enjoy premium shisha, and create lasting memories in an elegant setting.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Berwick Manor was born from a passion for fine dining and a desire to offer a unique experience. We envisioned a place where the art of cooking is celebrated alongside the tradition of shisha, all within an ambiance that exudes sophistication and warmth.

Our Mission

To provide an unparalleled dining and leisure experience. We are committed to using the freshest ingredients, crafting innovative dishes, and delivering exceptional service. We aim to create a welcoming environment where every guest feels valued and leaves with a desire to return.

Our Values

Guided by quality, creativity, and community, we are dedicated to continuous improvement, embracing innovation, and fostering a culture of respect and collaboration among our team and guests.
